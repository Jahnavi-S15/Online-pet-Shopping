Dogs, known as man's best friend, are loyal, intelligent, four-legged mammals descended from wolves, valued for their companionship, protection, and ability to sense human emotions. These adaptable animals, which come in hundreds of diverse breeds, possess keen senses of smell and hearing, making them useful in roles such as working animals for police and military, therapy animals, and beloved family pets. Their presence provides significant psychological benefits, including stress reduction, while their playful nature encourages activity and well-being in their human companions.
